01 — ABOUT

Why data, why me

I'm a final-year Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering student turned aspiring data analyst — the kind of person who can't look at a spreadsheet without wanting to pivot it.

My work sits at the intersection of data storytelling and problem-solving: cleaning messy datasets, running exploratory analysis, and packaging the findings into dashboards that a non-technical stakeholder can act on in thirty seconds.
